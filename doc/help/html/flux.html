Flux Sources

Extrusion Rate

The decimal value can be entered in this text field. The rate at which material extrudes vertically from the ground. The field cannot be left blank.

Active Time Start

The decimal value can be entered in this text field. Specify a starting time for the flux source that can encompass either part or all of the simulation. The field cannot be left blank.

Active Time End

The decimal value can be entered in this text field. Specify a ending time for the flux source. The field cannot be left blank.

Center of Source X

The decimal value, in UTM coordinates, can be entered in this text field. The field cannot be left blank.

Center of Source Y

The decimal value, in UTM coordinates, can be entered in this text field. The field cannot be left blank.

Major Extent

The decimal value can be entered in this text field. Required because the flux source is elliptically shaped. The field cannot be left blank.

Minor Extent

The decimal value can be entered in this text field. Required because the flux source is elliptically shaped. The field cannot be left blank.

Orientation Angle

The decimal value, in degrees from X axis to major axis, can be entered in this text field. The field cannot be left blank.

Initial Speed

The decimal value can be entered in this text field. The initial horizontal speed (i.e. tangential to the terrain) of the material as it leaves the flux-source (the flux-source itself, remains stationary). The field cannot be left blank.

Initial Direction

The decimal value, measured in degrees from the X axis, can be entered in this text field. The field cannot be left blank.
